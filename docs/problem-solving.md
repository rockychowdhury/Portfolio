### **3. Strategic Advice for Display**

* **Lead with Your Strongest Platform:** If you are a Codeforces Grandmaster, that should be your headline stat. If you have a 365-day LeetCode streak, highlight that consistency first.
* **Focus on Key Milestones:** Emphasize reaching titles (Expert, Master, etc.) or breaking specific point thresholds.
* **Quantify Context:** A high rank is great, but adding "Top 1% of over 100,000 users" adds powerful context.
* **Keep It Updated:** This is dynamic information. A stat from two years ago is far less impressive than a high rating today.

By strategically showcasing these statistics, you transform from someone who "just codes" into a proven, algorithmic problem solver.
